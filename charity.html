Charity

#UnboundCharityWeek

Having a community that support transgender people is incredibly important to us as a team. Therefore, we decided to dedicate the final week (Monday-Friday) of every month to raise money for transgender and LGBTQ+ charities.

We plan on doing at least one 6-hour charity livestream during this week as well as other events to raise money for the charities.

Check out the charities we have supported and plan to support in the future below.

November

Mermaids UK

26th - 30th November 2018

Mermaids is passionate about supporting children, young people, and their families to achieve a happier life in the face of great adversity. We work to raise awareness about gender nonconformity in children and young people amongst professionals and the public. We campaign for the recognition of gender dysphoria in young people and lobby for improvements in professional services.

October

The Trevor Project

$100 raised in 24 hours

The Trevor Project is an American non-profit organization founded in 1998 focused on suicide prevention efforts among lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and questioning youth. Through a toll-free telephone number, it operates The Trevor Lifeline, a confidential service that offers trained counsellors.

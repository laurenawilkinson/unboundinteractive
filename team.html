About Us

Unbound Interactive is a games development company based in Stoke-on-Trent. The three of us are all students at Staffordshire university school of computing. We met during our time at university and became good friends working together on many projects and playing many hours of Super Smash Brothers.

We became close as we learned about each other and helped each other with our transitions and discovering of our sexualities. Earlier this year we talked about our idea to create our own company, with us being passionate about video games and two of us being on a video game programming course at university we decided that should be what we work on.

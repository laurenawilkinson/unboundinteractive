What is Transhaping?

Transhaping is a non-binary inclusive transgender awareness platform-puzzle game. You play as a shape who is struggling to accept who they are. The player gets to experience the shape find themselves and go through their journey of transition and tackle many struggles along the way. The player can choose the characters transition and pronouns from the start of the game.

Gameplay

The game itself will feature side-scrolling platforming and puzzle elements along with stealth game elements based around managing stress/dysphoria. As the player goes through the game their journey will unravel with a narrator overseeing the events and with the player character encountering other characters during their story.

While the gameplay is an important element, the narrative is crucial to us as a development team and we feel should be experienced regardless of skill level, so there will be options for a zero-stress mode allowing players to experience the journey without fear of failure.

Story and Importance

The story has been constructed from the personal experiences of us (the development team) and that of our friends. We hope that by relating it to what we have gone through we will do the subject proud as it is a subject that is very near and dear to all of us and we feel an important experience to share with those curious or going through similar experiences.

It's important that no one going through a transition feels alone and we hope that this game will help others feel not alone and to know there are others out there going through the difficult process too.
